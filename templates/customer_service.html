Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Most orders are processed within 1–2 business days and arrive within 3–7 days depending on your location.

Can I return or exchange an item?

Yes! We accept returns within 7 days of delivery as long as the item is unused and in its original packaging.

What if my plant arrives damaged?

We guarantee healthy delivery. If your plant arrives damaged, send us a photo and we’ll replace it for free.

Do you offer care instructions?

Absolutely. Each plant includes care instructions, and our BlooomyBot page can help answer plant‑care questions.

How do I track my order?

Track your order progress and history through our order history link!
